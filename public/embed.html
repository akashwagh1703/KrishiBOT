✨ Features:

  • 🌤️ Get real-time weather updates
  • 📜 Explore government schemes and subsidies
  • 🛡️ Diagnose plant diseases and get treatment recommendations
  • 🌾 Select crops and get disease-specific guidance
  • 💬 Chat with AI for farming advice

How to Embed KrishiBot

Simply add this script tag to your website:

<script src="https://your-domain.com/krishibot-widget.js"></script>
<script>
  KrishiBot.init({
    position: 'bottom-right',
    theme: 'light',
    language: 'en'
  });
</script>

👉 Click the chat button in the bottom-right corner to start!
